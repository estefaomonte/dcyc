DCYC's Program and Activities

Pre-Congress

    It happened last November 23, 2017 attended by participants coming from different sectors: In-school youth, out of school, working youth, community-based, religious-faithy based, LGBT, and fraternity youth. Election therewith was also raised, and the birth of DCYC afterwhich existed.

 DCYC goes RAFI-KAC

It was on January 31 to February 1 and 2, 2018 when the Danao City Youth Commissioners together with the youth representatives from each barangay came along to Ramon Aboitiz Foundation Incorporated- Kool Adventure Camp (RAFI-KAC). There, their connections as a team were strengthened as they face the different dragons altogether. #SIDLAKDANAO!

 The Youth Congress and other incoming activities

On March 2018, the DCYC will conduct a YOUTH CONGRESS. Every youth can be a participant to this undertaking. For more information, kindly visit  Danao City Youth Commission fb page. Thank you.
